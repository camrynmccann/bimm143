Lets use a bioinformatics method called NMA(Normal Mode Analysis) to predict the dynamics (flexibility) of this enzyme

modes <- nma(pdb)
##  Building Hessian...     Done in 0.031 seconds.
##  Diagonalizing Hessian...    Done in 0.115 seconds.
plot(modes)

Make a trajectory! (movie of its predicted motion).

mktrj(modes, file="nma.pdb")
